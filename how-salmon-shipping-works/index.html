How It Works:

Our Local CSF Pick-ups vs. Door-to-Door Shipping Explained.

1. You decide: Door-to-door shipping vs. Local CSF Pick-up.

Option 1: Door-to-Door Shipping. Our premium salmon shows up conveniently at your front door frozen in 10lb quantities for a flat $199. Shipments begin in September following the summer salmon harvest. No shipping costs or hidden fees. BLAH BLAH.
Option 2: Local CSF Pick-ups. For those of you near a local CSF pickup location, these September events are the most economical way to obtain this premium salmon.

2. Purchase Your Salmon Share

Like fruit and veggie CSA’s, it starts with a pre-season online purchase of a share to guarantee your order. We do not provide salmon year-round since it is only harvested in the summer. Annual spring orders fund necessities like professional processing and frozen freight, and once it is sold-out, it is gone until the next year. While our customers gain access to high quality wild fish and the fisherman who harvested it, they also give support to the fishery itself, because small-boat fishermen are invested for the long term in the vitality and sustainability of the salmon and the pristine habitat in which they thrive.

3. We Go Fishing

The annual sockeye salmon run in Bristol Bay runs mid-June to late-July. We use small boats and gill-nets to fish near the mouth of the Kvichak River. The hand-picked bounty is transferred quickly to larger tender vessels to chill en route to the professional processors who work nearby on the fishing grounds.

4. Your Salmon is Fileted & Packaged

The best of the harvest is hand selected for filets. All bones, including pin bones, are removed and each filet is individually vacuum packaged with skin on one side. Rapid processing ensures just-caught flavor and freshness. Immediate and sustained deep freezing makes each filet sushi-grade, for raw consumption.

5. We Manage the Logistics

In August, the frozen-once salmon shares travel by barge from Alaska to the port in Seattle, Washington. From there they are prepared for shipment to your front door by the end of September, OR they are prepared for our local CSF Pick-up events depending upon how you ordered. For our customers who use our door-to-door shipping, specialized packaging, and dry ice ensure that your salmon arrives frozen at your front door. For our local CSF Customers, our bulk frozen shipping logisitics and September pick-up events ensure premium quality salmon at competitive pricing.

6. Enjoy!

We’ll provide thawing and cooking best practices and some recipes to get you started, though it takes very little to achieve delicious results. According to the USDA, our salmon is good for up to two years (when kept frozen), but it tends to disappear long before then!
